Early EdTech GTM Leadership at IILV (1999–2003)

🔹 Challenge

IILV needed to sell the concept of e-learning in a pre-digital India market. The category didn’t exist. GTM was undefined.

🔹 Approach

Built the product strategy, ran field sales across schools and enterprises, created pricing models, and built a 10-person GTM team from scratch.

🔹 Outcome

Became one of India’s first successful edtech products. Created lasting GTM IP, validated enterprise edtech before the sector was popular.

““We were 10 years too early — and still made it work. GTM was ground-up, gritty, and pioneering.””
