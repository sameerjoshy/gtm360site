Case Study 5 — Channel Partner Strategy for Growth

Challenge:

Company was entering cost-sensitive markets but had a direct-sales-heavy motion that lacked reach and efficiency.

Approach:

Designed a full channel strategy — from partner profiling to onboarding to incentives. Identified, recruited, and enabled 12+ key partners.

Outcome:

40% of revenue in those markets now comes via partners. CAC dropped. Partner managers run a quarterly review system.

Quote:

“They didn’t just find partners — they built a program. We now have a real partner-led motion.”
