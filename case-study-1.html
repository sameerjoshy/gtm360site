Case Study 1 — Scaling a $50M Business to $1B (AWS)

Challenge:

When the AWS business unit was still early stage (~$50M revenue), it lacked structure in its GTM segmentation, tooling, and regional execution planning.

Approach:

We built a multi-segment GTM strategy tailored for Startups, SMBs, and Enterprise accounts. Created vertical-specific motions, mapped account plans by region, and implemented scalable RevOps systems.

Outcome:

Revenue scaled from $50M to $1B+ in 5 years. Org grew from 35 to 400+ people. RevOps became a global center of excellence.

Quote:

“They turned chaos into cadence. From market segmentation to hiring strategy — this team helped us scale fast without breaking.”
