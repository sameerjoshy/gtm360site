Case Study 3 — Building RevOps from Scratch (Innovapptive)

Challenge:

Innovapptive had strong enterprise traction but lacked the RevOps infrastructure to scale into mid-market and global territories.

Approach:

Established a Revenue Ops center of excellence in India. Rebuilt the full commercial engine (lead gen to CS) and implemented Salesforce, Outreach, Gainsight, and dashboards.

Outcome:

90% YoY growth. Entered EU/APAC with a lite product. Hired a global partner manager and expanded via channel.

Quote:

“RevOps wasn’t a function — it became a growth engine. We were flying blind before.”
