Case Study 2 — APAC GTM Expansion (AWS)

Challenge:

AWS was expanding rapidly into Korea, ASEAN, and ANZ markets — but lacked localized GTM playbooks and partner strategies.

Approach:

Defined region-specific GTM models with geo headcount planning, partner capability mapping, and pipeline coverage ratios. Delivered these through a regional RevOps overlay.

Outcome:

Established scalable GTM motions in 3 markets. Partner-sourced revenue grew 3x. Region transitioned from reactive to proactive pipeline planning.

Quote:

“Helped us localize and lead. Their RevOps and partner strategy let us play offense in every market.”
