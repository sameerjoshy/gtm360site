Case Study 4 — Funnel & Segmentation Overhaul (Startup)

Challenge:

This VC-backed SaaS startup had strong inbound traffic but poor pipeline quality and leaky conversion rates.

Approach:

Conducted a full-funnel audit, redefined ICPs, cleaned CRM data, and deployed a new scoring model. Refreshed messaging across campaigns.

Outcome:

Conversion rate improved 35% in 3 months. Average deal size increased 20%. SDR team had better prioritization and morale.

Quote:

“They helped us work smarter, not harder. Focus and clarity changed everything.”
