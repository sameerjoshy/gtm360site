Case Study 6 — Sales-Product Alignment for Expansion

Challenge:

Mid-market teams were struggling to penetrate new verticals and feature adoption was stagnant.

Approach:

Ran a voice-of-customer study, worked with product on roadmap gaps, and co-built new vertical solution messaging. Enabled sellers via playbooks and workshops.

Outcome:

Expansion revenue grew 2.2x. Product satisfaction scores increased. CSAT rose across 3 core segments.

Quote:

“The sales-product flywheel was finally real. Everyone started speaking the same language — customers included.”
